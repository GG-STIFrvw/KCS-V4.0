Why KCS Auto Repair Shop?

We offer on-demand, casa-quality car maintenance services and secondhand car buying inspections, making car care convenient and accessible.

Billing / Payment / Refund

You can pay online via our website using credit cards, GCash, Maya, other e-wallets, or online bank transfer, even after the service is completed.

Pricing

You can request an estimate online by selecting your location, car and services. In most cases, we will give you a price instantly. You will see the breakdown of labor fees and parts costs.

Scheduling

You can book an appointment for a mechanic to come out to you and inspect your car. They will provide their inspection report and recommend services that your car needs.

You can easily book a service through our website or social media pages. Follow these simple steps to book via our website:

    1. Visit our website: Go to kcsautorepairshop.com

    2. Select your service, and choose from our three main services:

  • Secondhand Car Buying Inspection
  • PMS-Oil Change Packages
  • Initial Diagnosis

    • 3. Enter vehicle details: Select your mileage/odometer range for PMS services

    4. Fill in your information: Provide accurate customer details

    5. Review and confirm: Double-check details and payment method

    6. Secure your booking: If paying later, a GCash QR code will appear

    7. Booking confirmation: A service advisor will confirm your booking
