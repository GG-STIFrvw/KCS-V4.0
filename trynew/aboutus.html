About KCS Auto Repair Shop

KCS Auto Repair Shop was established in February 2010 and is situated on an 800 sq mtr area at #56-A Kanlaon Street, Brgy Sta Teresita, Quezon City. The premises are well-secured with concrete walls, ensuring safety for our operations and clients' vehicles.

KCS Auto Repair Shop prides itself on uncompromisingly high standards of workmanship and quality repairs. Our services are backed by professional management, a team of skilled and competent workers, and state-of-the-art tools and equipment to meet the repair needs of our clients and guarantee customer satisfaction.
